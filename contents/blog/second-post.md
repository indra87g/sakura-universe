# Exciting New Features in the Survival World!

Hello everyone! We've been hard at work improving the survival experience on Sakura Universe. We're excited to announce a few new features that are now live on the server.

## Custom Enchantments

We have added a set of new, custom enchantments to the game. These enchantments can be obtained from the enchanting table, just like vanilla enchantments. They offer a range of new abilities and perks to make your survival journey more exciting.

## New Dungeons

Explore the world and you might stumble upon new, challenging dungeons. These dungeons are filled with custom mobs and offer unique rewards for those brave enough to conquer them.

### The Sunken Tomb

A new underwater dungeon with a powerful boss at the end.

### The Cursed Forest

A dark and spooky forest with many secrets to uncover.

We hope you enjoy these new features. As always, we welcome your feedback and suggestions. Happy crafting!
